Join Slack, this club's main communication tool!

Instructions for Joining Slack

  1. Go to https://slack.hackclub.com/ and add your email.
  2. Go to your email and join the Slack group.
  3. Once your Slack page loads, go to the left bar and find the portion called "Channels". Click the + button and search for the "mason" channel. Join that and you'll be all set for communication within the club!
