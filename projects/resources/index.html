Codecademy

Pro: Easy to use, good user interface, fun/interactive, wclasse variety of languages to learn

Con: Skips content, sometimes difficult to understand

W3Schools

Pro: Enormous library of information, easy to maneuver, very in depth, "Try it yourself!" editor allows interaction

Con: Less interactive, no lessons; rather, just pages of information

Hack Club Workshops

Pro: Great lessons, clear direction, self-directed learning, introduction to real applications, finishing a workshop ends with a real product (website, game, etc.)

Con: Requires some installation of programs sometimes, not interactive
