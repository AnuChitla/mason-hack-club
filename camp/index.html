About Hack Camp

What is Hack Camp?

Have you ever wondered how to make a website? Do you want to learn more about how to code? Hack Camp is the way to go!

In this fun four-day camp, you’ll learn about the basics of website development by starting out with HTML, CSS, and Javascript to build your own personal websites. Along with your peers, you will learn, hands-on, how to code and will be introduced to some basic programming logic. Students will leave the camp with their own websites built from scratch!

Hack Camp will be taught by Mason Hack Club volunteers and organizers, all of whom have experience in programming as well as web design.

When is Hack Camp?

Hack Camp will be running from 12:30pm to 5pm, June 27th to June 30th.

Address: 5191 Natorp Blvd, Mason, OH 45040

Hack Camp costs $130 for the four-day program. If financial aid is required, please email us at masonhackclub@gmail.com.

Who can come?

Hack Camp is open to Mason students! Specifically, we're targeting incoming 6th graders to incoming 9th graders. If you're older and interested in attending, please reach out to us at masonhackclub@gmail.com.

FAQs

Isn't hacking bad?

For some people, the word "hack" is associated with things like breaking into bank accounts. However, that's not really hacking. When we say hack, we mean finding a creative solution to a problem (think of life hacks!) using technology--no banks involved!

What will I do?

We'll be taking you through a course where you learn to create your very own website. After that, you'll have the opportunity to split into groups and design a website to market a product! We'll be including fun, non-coding activities to keep you from burning out.

I don't know how to code!

Great! The curriculum we've designed puts new coders in mind. We have tutorials to walk you through getting set up, and our volunteers will be there to help you

I know how to code!

Great! Hack Camp will be a great place for you to refine your skills. If you don't know HTML, CSS, or Javascript, then this will be an opportunity to more to your portfolio.

What do I need?

Bring your laptop to code on! Additionally, we won't be serving lunch or dinner, so bring a snack if you'd like.
