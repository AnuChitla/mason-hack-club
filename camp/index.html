When is Hack Camp?

Hack Camp will be running from 1pm-5pm, June 26th-June 30th.

Where is Hack Camp?

We'll be renting out the B1 computer labs in Mason High School.

How much is Hack Camp?

Hack Camp costs $130 for the five-day program. However, if financial aid is required, email us at masonhackclub@gmail.com.

Who can come?

Hack Camp is open to Mason students! Specifically, we're targeting incoming 6th graders to incoming 9th graders. However, if you're older and interested in attending, reach out to us at masonhackclub@gmail.com.

FAQs

Isn't hacking bad?

For some people, the word "hack" is associated with things like breaking into bank accounts. However, that's not really hacking. When we say hack, we mean finding a creative solution to a problem (think of life hacks!) using technology--no banks involved!
